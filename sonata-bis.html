Deep Learning for Tabular Data

NCN Sonata-Bis Project (2023/50/E/ST6/00169)

Motivation
In real-world applications, the most common data type is tabular data. However, deep learning models are mostly designed for CV and NLP tasks, which represent only a subset of real-life data. Even though practical applications are dominated by tabular data, deep learning models do not confirm the state-of-theart performance in this domain.
Goal
Motivated by the importance of tabular data in real-life problems, we aim at transferring the successful deep learning story from CV and NLP to the case of tabular data.
